Exercises


As I have already mentioned, there are countless exercises. Basically, anybody is capable of making a workout plan, with just a bit of effort and research, and with experience, you will be able to tune the variesty of these exercises that fits you the best. However, there are exercises, that are recommended rather for experienced trainees, but in my opinion, with good form it doesn’t matter how long you have been training. Besides a lot depends on how much time you have for training, and how much rest your body demands. In this article I’m going to show you my 3 day split workout. I will skip cardio exercises, because I do different sports, and I count them as cardio. But in case you want tod o some, I recommend running/jogging, swimming or jumpropes. (Just as benchmark I’m 22 and I’d consider myself as middle experienced in training). Fortunately, with this split you don’t have to worry organizing your rest days. Ont he one hand, there are four of them, and thats considered to be a lot. ont he other hand if you do them in the right order, you can do them even on consecutive days. Moreover, if you want to train more you can just continou by starting it again from the first day.

Muscle groups:

  • Biceps
  • Triceps
  • Back
  • Legs
  • Chest
  • Abs
  • Shoulders

Biceps:

You will be doing it in superset with Triceps. A videó leírása
  1. Exercise: dumbbell curl – superset with dips
  2. Exercise: spider curls (video 2) - superset superset with dumbbel skullcrusher, narrow benchpress
  3. Exercise: cross body hammer curl (video 3)- superset with overhead extension
  4. Incline dumbbel curl (video 4)-superset with one hand cable pushdown

Triceps:

  1. Exercise: dips (video5)
  2. Exercise: dumbbel skullcrusher with narrow bench press (video6,7)
  3. Exercise: overhead extension (video8)
  4. Exercise:one hand cable push down (video9)

Back:

You will do it on the same day with shoulders, but you have to start with back exercises.
  1. Exercise: lat pull ups with machine rows (video10,11)
  2. Exercise: neutral grip pull ups (video12)
  3. Exercise: cable push down(video13)
  4. Exercise: bent over row (video14)

Legs:

You will do it on the same day with chest, but you have to start with chest exercises.
  1. Exercise: leg extension (video15)
  2. Exercise: deadlift (video16)
  3. Exercise: squats (video17)
  4. Exercise:hip thrust (video18)
Most of the time I do 1 calf exercise, but thats optional.

Chest:

  1. Exercise: incline dumbbel benchpress (video19)
  2. Exercise: bench press (video20)
  3. Exercise: chest fly(video21)
  4. Exercise:cable crossover (video22)

Abs:

Try to do them 3x a week at the beginning of any training.
  1. Exercise: Cable crunches (video23)
  2. Exercise: cable side crunches (video24)
  3. Exercise: leg raise (video25)

Shoulders:

  1. Exercise: overhead dumbbelpress (video26)
  2. Exercise: reverse chest fly (video27)
  3. Exercise: lateral raises (video28)
  4. Exercise:upright row (video29)

Try to do all the exercise with as much weight as you can rep for 8 in 4 sets. For the last set, try it with a bigger weight and do a dropset (drop the 1/3 of the weight 2 times and do as much reps as you can). Hope it will be helpful to begin your journey as a gymshark.
